Overview


Skills on how to take of plants and the way we use the tools, cutting and trimming to make the garden neat. Make sure to use it in the right so everyone is safe. Gardening maintenance courses involve core horticulture skills such as plant identification, soil care, and seasonal gardening duties. Students gain knowledge of the most important gardening tools and equipment, how to use them safely, and other related topics. Advanced themes such as landscape design, garden planning, and outdoor décor to improve the visual appeal of outdoor environments. Participants in garden maintenance courses are intended to learn correct safety procedures and ecologically responsible gardening.
